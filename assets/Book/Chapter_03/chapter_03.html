1. Formatting Elements

1.1 <b> (Bold)

Makes text bold without adding importance.

<b>Fahim</b>
Fahim

1.2 <i> (Italic)

Makes text italic, typically for technical terms or emphasis.

<i>Fahim</i>
Fahim

1.3 <u> or <ins> (Underline)

Underlines text using <u> or represents inserted text with <ins>.

<u>Fahim</u> or <ins>Fahim</ins>
Fahim or Fahim

1.4 <s> or <del> (Strikethrough)

Shows deleted or no longer relevant content.

<s>Fahim</s> or <del>Fahim</del>
Fahim or Fahim

1.5 <mark> (Highlight)

Highlights text for reference or search match.

<mark>Fahim</mark>
Fahim

1.6 <sub> and <sup> (Subscript & Superscript)

<sub> for subscript (e.g., H2O) and <sup> for superscript (e.g., x2).

H<sub>2</sub>O and x<sup>2</sup>
H2O and x2

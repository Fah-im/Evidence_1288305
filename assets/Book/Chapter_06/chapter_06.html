1. Audio

The <audio> element is used to embed sound content in documents.

<audio controls>
  <source src="vocal-funny-362402.mp3" type="audio/mpeg">
  Your browser does not support the audio element.
</audio>

2. Video

The <video> element is used to embed video content in documents.

<video width="320" height="240" controls>
  <source src="mixkit-children-playing-with-a-dancing-fountain-3469-hd-ready.mp4" type="video/mp4">
  Your browser does not support the video tag.
</video>
